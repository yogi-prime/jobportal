- Drag-and-drop resume creation interface

**How to use:**
1. Visit Job Matcher page
2. Answer 4 questions about:
   - Your skills
   - Work preferences
   - Experience level
   - Career interests
3. Get instant personalized matches
4. Apply to recommended jobs

#### 3. 💰 Salary Calculator
**What it does:**
- Calculate your market value

- Industry benchmarks and data
- Negotiation tips and strategies
- Skills impact analysis (see how skills affect salary)

**How to use:**
1. Go to Salary Calculator
2. Select your:
   - Job title/role
   - Years of experience
   - Location
   - Skills
3. Get instant salary estimate
4. View negotiation tips

#### 4. 📅 Interview Scheduler
**What it does:**
- Schedule interviews with built-in calendar
- View available time slots
- Automatic timezone conversion
- Email confirmations sent automatically
- Reschedule easily
- Google Calendar integration support

**How to use:**
1. Receive interview invitation
2. Visit Interview Scheduler
3. Pick available time slot
4. Confirm booking
5. Receive email confirmation

#### 5. 📧 Job Alerts
**What it does:**
- Get notified of new jobs matching your criteria

  - Keywords

  - Category
- Unsubscribe anytime

**How to use:**
